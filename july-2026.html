We wanted to let you know about an important new initiative. Harland will be launching a Teacher Training Program that all teachers will be expected to complete.
Built around three pillars
1
Understanding Harland's culture.
2
Strengthening foundational teaching practices.
3
Developing skills that support the standard of education our families expect.

As our team grows, we want to ensure that everyone is working from the same playbook with a shared understanding of who we are and how we work.

Curriculum &
Unit Completion
When you take on a student
When you are assigned a student as their primary teacher, please check that you have the student's curriculum and writing document. If either is missing, send a message in your teacher LINE group so Admin can get it to you. The same applies whenever you cover a substitute class.
At the end of every unit
Every curriculum has four units, and each unit concludes with a writing project (for English) or a test (for Science and Math). These are a required part of the curriculum, not an optional extra. Every student is expected to complete the writing project or test at the end of each unit. They are central to how we teach at Harland and how we show families the real progress their child is making.
Once a unit is complete, notify Admin in your LINE group with the student's name and the writing project or test result. Admin will then send a unit completion to the parents, sharing their child's progress and areas for growth.
Unit audit
Due Fri, Jul 31
We will be auditing English writing projects completed since the beginning of the year. Please check the records for each student you are the primary English teacher of. Each student should have one writing project per unit completed. Please also ensure that grades and comments have been filled in, and that all fillable sections have been reviewed and completed. If anything is missing, send it through by Friday, July 31, in the format below, one message per project, in your teacher LINE group.
Reminder · all subjects
As a reminder for all subjects: when your student completes Lesson 11 of a unit, please notify Admin in your teacher LINE group and send through the student's completed measurable (writing project, test, or assessment). This helps us keep everything on track and ensures families receive their child's progress update promptly.

When you finish a class, please make sure all computers and laptops are turned off, the air conditioning is off, the lights are off, and the door is left open. An open door is how the next teacher knows a room is available.

Moving
forward. Together.
As the summer term rolls on, we want to pause and say thank you for the new initiatives you have taken on, the new faces you have welcomed, and the steady care you bring to your students every day. None of it goes unnoticed.

We know this month brings new expectations, from the Teacher Training Program to the unit audit. These changes are not about adding work for the sake of it. They are about bringing us closer together around a shared vision for how we teach, how we grow, and the standard we hold ourselves to. When we are aligned, every student and every family benefits.

You are a big part of why Harland feels like Harland. The warmth, the high standards, the real investment in every child, all of that comes from you. There is real strength in a team that moves in the same direction, and that is exactly what we are building here.

Keep going, and keep bringing your best. We are grateful to have you on this team, and we are excited for everything ahead.
