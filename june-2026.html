A few friendly reminders to help scheduling run smoothly for everyone.
1
Keep your Teachworks availability up to date. This streamlines the scheduling process.

When you're offered a class, a quick reply goes a long way. It helps us confirm things with families sooner.
3
No penalty for declining a class. We work around your schedule. All we need is a prompt reply so we can plan accordingly.
